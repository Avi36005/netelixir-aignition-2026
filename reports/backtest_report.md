## 6. Known limitations

- WAPE ~72% at *campaign* level reflects genuinely volatile per-campaign
  revenue over 30–90 day horizons; aggregate (channel/blended) medians
  reconcile bottom-up and are correspondingly steadier.
- Coverage runs ~10 pts above the 80% target — the calibrator prefers the
  conservative side; wide intervals are honest, not a bug.
- MAPE is reported for completeness but is a misleading headline on data with
  many near-zero actuals; lead with WAPE + coverage.
- One BFCM peak in the history limits how sharp the seasonal index can be.
